General Email Delivery Considerations in AJO

Email delivery issues can stem from several areas:

  • Email channel configuration – Ensure the email channel has been properly set up
  • Sender authentication – Verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records are configured
  • Subdomain delegation – Check that subdomains are properly delegated for sending
  • Suppressions and exclusions – Review if profiles are on suppression lists or excluded from the journey
  • Journey logic – Verify conditions and entry criteria aren't preventing message sends
  • Content validation – Check that email content passes validation before sending
  • Frequency capping – Confirm frequency caps aren't limiting sends
