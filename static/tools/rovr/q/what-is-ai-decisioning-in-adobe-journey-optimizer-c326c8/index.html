What is AI decisioning in Adobe Journey Optimizer?

AI Decisioning in Adobe Journey Optimizer

AI Decisioning is Adobe Journey Optimizer's advanced capability that empowers you to deliver personalized marketing offers by combining a centralized catalog of decision items with a powerful decision engine. It represents the next generation of decisioning functionality, shifting from legacy decision management to a more intelligent, schema-based approach.

Key Capabilities

Intelligent Offer Selection - AI models rank and prioritize offers to display for specific customer profiles, moving beyond traditional priority scores to use advanced machine learning and data points.

AI-Powered Ranking - The trained model system leverages multiple data points to determine which offer should be presented first for a given placement, optimizing based on your business goals rather than static rules.

Multi-Channel Support - Currently available via:

  • Code-based experiences
  • Email channels

AI Model Types

AI Decisioning includes two primary model approaches:

1. Auto-optimization - Focuses on improving overall performance globally based on historical data, aiming for metrics like clicks and conversions

2. Personalized optimization - Tailors recommendations for individual customers and specific audiences, providing audience-specific insights

How It Works

You can create different AI models based on your business objectives. For example, you might select one AI model for the email channel and another for push campaigns. For each channel, the decisioning engine analyzes customer context and data to automatically select the optimal offer without relying solely on predefined priority scores.

This schema-based approach enables dynamic item rendering and introduces experimentation into decisioning, helping brands use available data and customer intelligence to optimize business value at scale.
