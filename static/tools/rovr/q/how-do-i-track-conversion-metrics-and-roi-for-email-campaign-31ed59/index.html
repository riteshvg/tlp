How do I track conversion metrics and ROI for email campaigns in Adobe Journey Optimizer?

Tracking Conversion Metrics & ROI for Email Campaigns in Adobe Journey Optimizer

Overview

Adobe Journey Optimizer provides multiple ways to track conversions and measure ROI for email campaigns. This involves configuring custom success metrics, setting up journey KPIs, and leveraging reporting capabilities to measure business outcomes beyond standard engagement metrics.

Step 1: Define Your Conversion Goals

Before setting up tracking, clearly define what constitutes a conversion for your email campaign:

| Conversion Type | Example |

|---|---|

| Purchase/Transaction | Customer completes a purchase after clicking email |

| Sign-up/Registration | Customer registers for an event or service |

| Renewal | Customer renews a subscription |

| Form Submission | Customer fills out a lead generation form |

| App Download | Customer downloads your mobile app |

| Content Engagement | Customer watches a video or reads an article |

Step 2: Configure Custom Success Metrics

Prerequisites

  • A Customer Journey Analytics (CJA) license is required for custom success metrics
  • Required dataset field groups must be added:
  • Commerce Details
  • Web
  • Mobile

How to Set Up Custom Success Metrics

1. Navigate to your Journey in Adobe Journey Optimizer

2. Open the Journey Properties

3. Locate the Success Metrics section

4. Click Add Metric

5. Select from the available metric types:

  • Commerce metrics (purchases, revenue, orders)
  • Web metrics (page views, form submissions)
  • Mobile metrics (app opens, in-app purchases)

6. Define the metric name and attribution model

7. Save your journey properties

> ⚠️ Important Note: Custom success metrics are currently available for journeys only, not standalone campaigns. For campaigns, use the standard KPI dashboard combined with CJA integration.

Step 3: Track Email Conversion Metrics

Available Conversion-Related Metrics in Email Reports

When you access your Email Campaign Report or Journey Email Report, the following conversion-related metrics are available:

+++ Click to expand Conversion Metrics

  • [!UICONTROL Clicks]: Number of times a content was clicked in your emails — the primary indicator of intent to convert.
  • [!UICONTROL Click-through Rate (CTR)]: Percentage of delivered emails that resulted in a click — measures how effectively your email drives action.
  • [!UICONTROL Click-through Open Rate (CTOR)]: Percentage of email openers who clicked — measures content relevance to engaged readers.
  • [!UICONTROL Unique Clicks]: Number of unique profiles who clicked at least once — avoids inflating conversion counts from repeat clicks by the same user.
  • [!UICONTROL Tracked Link URLs]: Individual performance of each CTA or link in your email — helps identify which specific offers or content drove conversions.

+++

Step 4: Set Up Journey Tracking for End-to-End Conversion

Enable Journey Step Events

Journey step events provide granular tracking of every action customers take as they move through your journey:

1. Go to Journey Settings

2. Enable Journey Step Events

3. This activates detailed tracking of:

  • Entry points — where customers enter the journey
  • Path selection — which journey paths customers follow
  • Drop-off locations — where customers exit without converting
  • Conversion points — where customers complete desired actions

Configure Send-Time Optimization Tracking

1. In Journey Settings, configure Send-Time Optimization

2. This tracks performance across different timing strategies

3. Helps identify optimal send windows that drive higher conversions

Step 5: Track ROI Using Reporting

Access Campaign Reports

1. Navigate to your Campaign in AJO

2. Click the [!UICONTROL Reports] button

3. Select [!UICONTROL View all time report]

4. Review the following ROI-relevant sections:

Email KPI Dashboard

The KPI dashboard provides a consolidated view of:

| Metric | ROI Relevance |

|---|---|

| Delivered | Total addressable audience reached |

| Click-through Rate | Intent and engagement signal |

| Unique Clicks | Unique conversion opportunities |

| Tracked Link Performance | Which CTAs drove the most action |

| Bounce Rate | List quality and deliverability health |

Delivered vs Click Trend Graph

The [!UICONTROL Delivered vs Click trend] graph presents a detailed analysis of your profiles' engagement with your emails, offering valuable insights into how various domains interact with your content:

  • [!UICONTROL Delivered]: Number of emails successfully sent in relation to the total number of sent emails
  • [!UICONTROL Clicks]: Number of times content was clicked in your emails

Step 6: Export Data for Deeper ROI Analysis

Share Data with External Systems

AJO allows you to export tracking data to external systems for deeper ROI analysis:

1. Navigate to Reports

2. Use the Export Data functionality to push data to:

  • Adobe Customer Journey Analytics (CJA) — for advanced attribution modeling
  • Adobe Analytics — for cross-channel reporting
  • External BI tools — via data export APIs

Build Custom Reports

1. In AJO Reports, select Create Custom Report

2. Add the metrics relevant to your ROI calculation:

  • Revenue-related metrics (via Commerce Details field group)
  • Engagement metrics (clicks, opens)
  • Delivery metrics (delivered, bounces)

3. Apply filters and segments to analyze specific audience cohorts

4. Schedule the report to run automatically for ongoing ROI monitoring

Step 7: Apply Attribution Models

Understanding Attribution in AJO

When measuring conversions driven by email campaigns, attribution models determine how credit is assigned to touchpoints:

| Attribution Model | How It Works | Best For |

|---|---|---|

| Last Touch | Full credit to the last email interaction before conversion | Short sales cycles |

| First Touch | Full credit to the first email that initiated the journey | Awareness campaigns |

| Linear | Equal credit distributed across all email touchpoints | Multi-step nurture campaigns |

| Custom | Configurable via CJA license | Complex multi-channel journeys |

> ⚠️ Note: Advanced attribution models require a Customer Journey Analytics (CJA) license integrated with AJO.

Step 8: Monitor Apple Mail Privacy Protection Impact

> ⚠️ Critical Note for Open Rate & Conversion Tracking:

>

> Since Apple introduced Mail Privacy Protection (MPP), tracking pixels used to measure email opens may be impacted. Apple's Mail app pre-fetches emails, which can artificially inflate open rates.

>

> Recommendation: Focus on clicks and conversion metrics instead of open rates for more accurate ROI insights. Use:

> - Click-through Rate (CTR) as a primary engagement signal

> - Estimated Opens metric (available in AJO) which adjusts for automated opens triggered by mail servers

ROI Calculation Framework

Use the following framework to calculate email campaign ROI in AJO:

```

ROI = ((Revenue Generated - Campaign Cost) / Campaign Cost) × 100

Where:

  • Revenue Generated = Conversions × Average Order Value
  • Conversions = Unique Clicks × Conversion Rate (measured externally)
  • Campaign Cost = Creative + Platform + Audience costs

```

Metrics to Pull from AJO Reports

| Input | AJO Metric to Use |

|---|---|

| Total audience reached | Delivered count |

| Engaged audience | Unique Clicks count |

| Top converting content | Tracked Link URL performance |

| Delivery efficiency | Bounce Rate and Error Rate |

| Engagement trend | Delivered vs Click Trend graph |

Summary — Conversion & ROI Tracking Checklist

  • ✅ Define conversion goals before campaign launch
  • ✅ Configure required dataset field groups (Commerce, Web, Mobile)
  • ✅ Set up custom success metrics (requires CJA license)
  • ✅ Enable journey step events for granular path tracking
  • ✅ Monitor CTR and unique clicks as primary conversion signals
  • ✅ Use tracked link URLs to identify top-performing CTAs
  • ✅ Export data to CJA or external tools for advanced ROI analysis
  • ✅ Apply appropriate attribution models based on your sales cycle
  • ✅ Focus on click metrics over open rates due to Apple MPP impact
  • ✅ Schedule automated reports for ongoing ROI monitoring
