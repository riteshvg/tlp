Is there a guide for setting up AJO email channels?

Yes, there are several guides available for setting up Adobe Journey Optimizer (AJO) email channels:

Channel Configuration Resources

Getting Started with Channel Configuration

This guide covers the foundational steps to configure channels in Adobe Journey Optimizer, including technical parameters and advanced settings for optimal performance.

Set Up and Manage Channel Configurations

A detailed guide that walks you through creating, editing, deactivating, and monitoring channel configurations. This ensures proper technical parameters are in place and compliance requirements are met.

Guided Channel Setup

For a more streamlined approach, there's a guided setup process that provides step-by-step instructions and resources to efficiently configure your email channels.

Email-Specific Setup Tasks

Once your channels are configured, you'll also want to address:

  • Email Subdomain Delegation — Configure and delegate email subdomains to optimize deliverability and maintain your domain reputation
  • IP Warmup Plans — Create and execute IP warmup plans to establish sender reputation and enhance email deliverability
  • Email Deliverability Monitoring — Manage suppression lists, allowed lists, and retry mechanisms to optimize email performance and reputation

These resources work together to give you a complete email channel setup in AJO, from initial configuration through ongoing monitoring and optimization.
