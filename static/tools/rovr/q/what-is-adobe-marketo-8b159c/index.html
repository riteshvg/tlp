What is Adobe Marketo?

Adobe Marketo

Adobe Marketo is a powerful marketing automation platform that helps marketers master the art and science of digital marketing to engage customers and prospects.

Overview

[!DNL Marketo] is an end-to-end customer experience management (CXM) solution for marketing, advertising, analytics, and commerce. It enables you to:

  • Automate and manage activities across the entire customer lifecycle
  • CRM lead management — capture, nurture, and qualify leads
  • Customer engagement — deliver personalized experiences at scale
  • Account-based marketing — target high-value accounts with coordinated campaigns
  • Revenue attribution — track and measure marketing's impact on revenue

Key Capabilities

Marketo provides:

  • Email marketing automation — create and execute sophisticated email campaigns
  • Lead tracking and management — monitor prospect engagement and behavior
  • Web personalization — deliver targeted content experiences across your digital properties
  • Landing page management — create and optimize conversion-focused landing pages
  • Analytics and reporting — measure campaign performance and ROI

Integration with Adobe Experience Platform

Marketo integrates with Adobe Experience Platform in several ways:

  • Marketo Engage source connector — Ingest Marketo person data and activity records into Experience Platform
  • Marketo Engage destinations — Sync audiences and profiles from Experience Platform to Marketo
  • Marketo Munchkin tracking — Track user behavior on your web properties for lead generation
  • Custom actions in Journey Optimizer — Sync person and custom object data from journeys into Marketo Engage

These integrations enable unified customer data management and coordinated marketing automation across Adobe's ecosystem.
