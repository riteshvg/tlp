What is Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker?

Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker

Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker is an enterprise AI-powered teammate built for customer experience and marketing work. It's designed to keep your work moving, whether you're launching campaigns or responding to new signals.

Key Characteristics

Grounded in Your Context

  • CX Enterprise Coworker is grounded in your brand, business processes, and customer context
  • It continuously evaluates outcomes to ensure alignment with your goals
  • It understands your unique organizational needs and workflows

Workflow Intelligence

  • Routes approvals at the right moments in your processes
  • Drives coordinated actions across workflows to deliver the right experiences
  • Automates decision-making and task routing based on your business logic

Purpose

  • Removes friction from customer experience and marketing workflows
  • Enables faster campaign launches and response to market signals
  • Coordinates activities across teams and systems to ensure cohesive customer experiences

How It Works

CX Enterprise Coworker operates as an integrated AI teammate that:

1. Understands your customer data and business context

2. Makes intelligent decisions about workflow routing and approvals

3. Takes coordinated actions across your marketing and CX systems

4. Learns from outcomes to improve future decisions

Access

CX Enterprise Coworker is integrated with Adobe Experience Platform and works in conjunction with other Adobe products like Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, and Customer Journey Analytics to provide unified customer experience intelligence and automation.
