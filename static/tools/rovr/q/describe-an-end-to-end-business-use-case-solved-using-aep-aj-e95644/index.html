End-to-End Business Use Case: Personalized Customer Lifecycle Journey

The Business Challenge

A mid-size luxury fashion retailer faces critical business problems:

  • High cart abandonment rate: 65% of customers add items but don't purchase
  • Poor customer retention: 40% of customers never return after first purchase
  • Inefficient marketing spend: Sending generic emails to all customers regardless of their behavior or preferences
  • Siloed customer data: Customer information scattered across CRM, e-commerce, email platform, and loyalty system
  • Manual processes: Marketing team manually segments customers and creates email campaigns

Business Goal: Implement a unified customer data platform powered by AEP + AJO to deliver personalized, real-time journeys that increase conversion rates, customer lifetime value, and marketing ROI.

Step 2: Build Unified Identity Graph

Identity Service links all customer records:

```

Before Unification (Siloed Data):

─────────────────────────────────

CRM System: John Smith (CRM-12345)

E-commerce: john.smith@email.com → ECID: 98765432...

Loyalty Program: LOYALTY-MEMBER-789

Anonymous Website: ECID: 98765432... (not linked to CRM yet)

After AEP Identity Unification:

───────────────────────────────

[CRM-12345]

↕

[john.smith@email.com] ← PRIMARY

↕

[ECID: 98765432...]

↕

[LOYALTY-MEMBER-789]

RESULT: Single unified identity graph

All data points to ONE customer

```
