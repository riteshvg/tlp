What permissions and roles are required to manage AJO email channel presets?

Permissions and Roles for Managing AJO Email Channel Presets

Required Permission

To create, edit, and delete channel configurations (email presets), you must have the following permission:

Manage messages presets

This is the minimum permission required to perform these administrative tasks in Journey Optimizer.

Role Requirements

Who Can Grant Permissions?

Only users with System Administrator or Product Administrator roles can:

  • Grant or withdraw permissions
  • Assign roles to users
  • Create custom roles

The minimum administrative role that can manage permissions is a Product Administrator. System administrators have no restrictions and can manage all aspects of permissions and access.

Key Administrative Roles Overview

| Role | Primary Responsibilities | Can Manage Presets? |

|------|-------------------------|-------------------|

| System Administrator | Unrestricted access to all admin functions | ✅ Yes (can assign permissions) |

| Product Administrator | Product-level administration and user management | ✅ Yes (can assign permissions) |

| Administrator (Journey Optimizer) | Environment setup and access management, configure channels and message presets | ✅ Yes (with proper permissions) |

Access Control Framework

Journey Optimizer access control uses Adobe CX Enterprise Permissions, which leverages:

  • Roles — Collections of users sharing the same permissions and sandboxes
  • Permissions — Sets of rights and restrictions that authorize or deny access to features
  • Sandboxes — Isolated environments for testing and development
  • Object and Attribute-Based Access Control — Fine-grained control over specific resources

Pre-Existing Roles Available

Journey Optimizer comes with a range of pre-existing roles suited to different job functions. If these don't meet your organization's needs, you can also create custom roles to fine-tune access to specific functionalities and objects in the interface.

For detailed information about available pre-existing roles and their specific permissions, refer to the out-of-the-box product profiles documentation.
