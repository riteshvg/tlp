80. What is Send Time Optimization?

Send-Time Optimization (STO)

Send-Time Optimization is an AI-powered feature in Adobe Journey Optimizer that automatically determines the best time to send each individual email or push notification to maximize engagement.

How It Works

Send-Time Optimization uses machine learning to analyze each recipient's historical behavior (opens, clicks, engagement patterns) and predicts the optimal moment for that specific person to receive a message. Rather than sending all messages at a fixed time, STO customizes the send time per recipient.

Supported Channels

  • Email – within Journeys only
  • Push notifications – within Journeys only

Note: Campaigns, custom actions, and other channels are not supported.

Key Performance Metrics

  • Expected improvement: Approximately 2–10% increase in email click rate or push open rate across all optimized messages
  • Example: If your current email click rate is 5.0%, STO could increase it to as much as 5.5%
  • Individual sends: Benefits may not be observable on single, small-volume message sends due to sample size variability

Prerequisites & Timeline

  • Minimum requirement: At least 30 days of Email or Push usage in Journey Optimizer to collect sufficient behavioral data
  • Model maturation: Results continue to improve for up to 16 weeks
  • Model updates: Models are initially trained and scored weekly; after 16 weeks, they're retrained and rescored monthly

Optimization Targets

When configuring STO, choose what to optimize for:

  • Clicks – Best for most emails (when you want recipients to take an action)
  • Opens – Best for informational messages not intended to drive a specific action

Best Practices for Maximum Benefits

You're more likely to see greater benefits from STO when:

  • Existing journeys use fixed send times that aren't well-optimized
  • Consumer behavior (clicks and opens) varies by location and preferences
  • You apply STO to a larger fraction of your email and push messages
  • You set maximum wait times within the recommended 6–12 hour range

Night-Time Sends

Yes, STO can send push notifications at night if a user's behavior suggests they're most likely to engage then. To avoid night-time sends, use a morning send time with a short maximum wait window.
