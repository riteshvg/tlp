How would you meet regulatory requirements for capturing detailed metadata at each step of a journey and share it with downstream systems?

What the documentation confirms

The `executionMetadata` helper allows you to capture custom key-value pairs during journey execution. These pairs are stored in the Message Feedback Event Dataset under a standardized schema structure (`_experience.customerJourneyManagement.messageExecution.metadata`). This metadata can then be exported to external systems for tracking, analytics, and downstream processing purposes.

What it does NOT cover

The documentation does not specify:

  • How to configure or manage the export of metadata to specific downstream systems
  • Technical requirements for regulatory compliance frameworks or which regulations are supported
  • Methods for querying or transforming this metadata after capture
  • How to handle multi-brand governance of metadata collection
  • Integration patterns with external reporting or compliance platforms

Where to find additional guidance

For multi-brand regulatory and governance considerations, consult:

  • https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/experience-platform/landing/multi-region-multi-brand-whitepaper

Your implementation team should confirm with your compliance and data governance leads which specific regulatory requirements apply to your use case, and validate the 2 KB per-action limit meets your metadata volume needs.
