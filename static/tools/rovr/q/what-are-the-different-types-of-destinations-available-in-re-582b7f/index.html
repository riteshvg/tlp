What are the different types of destinations available in Real-Time CDP?

Types of Destinations in Real-Time CDP

Based on the documentation provided, destinations in Real-Time CDP are categorized as follows:

Connection vs. Extension Types

The documentation distinguishes between two main structural types:

  • Connections - Pre-built integrations that allow you to activate data to destination platforms
  • Extensions - Additional integrations available within the platform

Destination Use Cases

Real-Time CDP supports destinations across multiple use case categories:

  • Cross-channel marketing campaigns - Reach audiences across multiple channels
  • Email campaigns - Target users via email
  • Targeted advertising - Deliver personalized ads to known and unknown audiences
  • Personalization - Customize customer experiences
  • Ad targeting - Focus on specific audience segments
  • Marketing automation - Automate marketing workflows

Adobe Enterprise Solutions

Real-Time CDP also supports connections to other Adobe CX Enterprise solutions for:

  • Ad targeting
  • Personalization
  • Marketing automation
  • Customer experience management
