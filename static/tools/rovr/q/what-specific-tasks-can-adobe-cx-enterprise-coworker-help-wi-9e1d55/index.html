What specific tasks can Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker help with in marketing and customer experience?

Specific Tasks CX Enterprise Coworker Can Help With

Based on the documentation available to me, here are the specific tasks and capabilities described:

Data Validation (AA to CJA Migration)

CX Enterprise Coworker includes a validation skill that helps Analytics admins when upgrading from Adobe Analytics to Customer Journey Analytics. Specifically, it:

  • Automatically compares each dimension, metric, and trend individually across implementations
  • Compares all Adobe Analytics report suites against all Customer Journey Analytics data views
  • Generates AI-driven insights and recommendations to facilitate your upgrade
  • Automatically detects whether data is mapped through an Analytics source connector or two side-by-side implementations — no prior knowledge of your architecture required

Sandbox & Data Exploration

Coworker can quickly surface a full inventory of your AEP sandbox resources via natural language, such as:

  • Number of audiences, datasets, schemas, sources, destinations, and journeys
  • Details about specific audiences that already exist

Campaign and Journey Work

While the documentation confirms Coworker supports launching campaigns and responding to new signals, specific step-by-step task details for campaign creation within the retrieved documentation are limited.

Natural Language Interaction

Coworker Chat in Playground allows you to:

  • Learn and investigate through natural-language prompts
  • Refine work through conversational iteration
  • Explore sample prompts to quickly understand capabilities

The documentation notes that Coworker is designed to handle end-to-end customer experience workflows — from raw data insights to live campaign execution — entirely through conversation, covering Real-Time CDP and Adobe Journey Optimizer use cases.
