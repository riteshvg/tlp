Adobe Experience Platform + AI for Segmentation and Personalization

Adobe Experience Platform leverages AI capabilities to enable intelligent segmentation and personalization. Here's how these work together:

AI-Powered Segmentation

Edge Segmentation enables real-time audience evaluation and personalization:

  • Instant Evaluation: Segment definitions are evaluated instantaneously on the edge network, enabling same-page and next-page personalization use cases
  • Real-Time Processing: Requests are processed at scale on edge servers closest to where data was collected
  • Dynamic Metadata Loading: Edge servers dynamically load required metadata to evaluate segments in real-time

Key Capabilities:

  • Segment definitions can be evaluated immediately for personalization decisions
  • Supports both same-page (current interaction) and next-page (subsequent interaction) personalization scenarios
  • Primary identity requirement: Edge segmentation honors requests with one primary marked identity

CustomerAI Integration for Propensity Scoring

CustomerAI is Adobe Experience Platform's AI service that generates propensity scores to predict customer behavior:

How It Works:

  • Integrates directly into Adobe Experience Platform's AI services
  • Generates propensity scores that predict likelihood of customer actions
  • Outputs are accessible through pre-built APIs and dashboards

Applied to Personalization:

The propensity scores from CustomerAI can be used within:

  • Adobe Journey Optimizer - to refine customer journeys and tailor messaging
  • Adobe Real-Time CDP - to enhance audience segmentation and segment refinement
  • Custom marketing strategies based on predicted customer behavior

AI Assistant for Guidance

AEP includes an AI Assistant that helps you:

  • Navigate Experience Platform and Real-Time CDP concepts
  • Understand your data objects and usage
  • Get insights into your platform configuration

This combination enables marketers and developers to create sophisticated, data-driven personalization strategies based on real-time behavior and predictive insights.
