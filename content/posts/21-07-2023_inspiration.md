I have always stayed away from idolising people. I found it an unnecessary inclination. All this while, I drew inspiration from books, music, movies, but never people. But recently there are three individuals who are inspiring me with their actions and words. This post is about what inspires me

<!--more-->

These three individuals are from three different fields - cinema, cricket and philosophy

The first person that really piqued my curiosity and made me become a huge fan is Christopher Nolan. The second person in that order is M S Dhoni and the third person is Naval Ravikant.

Who inspires you? Or are your own self inspiration?
