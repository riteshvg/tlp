How do I create custom detail views for dashboards in Adobe Analytics?

Creating Custom Detail Views for Dashboards in Adobe Analytics

Custom detail views allow you to refine the story you tell stakeholders through mobile scorecard projects in Adobe Analytics Dashboards.

▶ Watch: Create Custom Detail Views for Dashboards

What Are Custom Detail Views?

Custom detail views are configurable drill-down screens within Adobe Analytics mobile scorecard projects. When an executive or stakeholder taps a tile in the mobile dashboard app, they are taken to a detail view — and you can customize what they see there, including:

  • Custom text to provide narrative context
  • Refined data visualizations
  • Tailored breakdowns relevant to the specific scorecard tile

How to Customize Detail Views

Prerequisites

  • You must have access to Adobe Analytics
  • You need an existing mobile scorecard project (or be creating a new one) in the Scorecard Builder
  • You should have Analyst or Admin level permissions to create/edit scorecards

Steps

1. Open the Scorecard Builder in Adobe Analytics by navigating to Workspace and selecting or creating a mobile scorecard project.

2. Select a tile on the scorecard canvas that you want to customize the detail view for.

3. Click on the detail view option associated with that tile — this opens the detail view editor.

4. Add or rearrange breakdown dimensions that will appear when a stakeholder drills into that tile from the mobile app.

5. Add custom text to the detail view to provide context, highlight key insights, or explain what the data means for the business.

6. Resize and reorder components within the detail view to control the narrative flow of the information.

7. Preview the detail view to confirm it communicates the story you intend for your stakeholders.

8. Save the scorecard — the customized detail views will be available to viewers using the Adobe Analytics dashboards mobile app.

Key Tips

  • Use custom text blocks strategically to guide non-technical stakeholders through the data story.
  • Keep detail views focused and concise — mobile screens have limited real estate.
  • Tailor breakdowns to the questions executives are most likely to ask when they see a particular metric tile.
