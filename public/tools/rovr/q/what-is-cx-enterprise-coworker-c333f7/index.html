What is CX Enterprise Coworker?

CX Enterprise Coworker

CX Enterprise Coworker is an enterprise AI-powered teammate designed specifically for customer experience and marketing work. It's built to help teams keep their work moving, whether they're launching campaigns or responding to new customer signals.

Key Capabilities

Context-Aware Intelligence

  • Grounded in your brand, business processes, and customer context
  • Continuously evaluates outcomes to ensure alignment with your goals

Workflow Automation

  • Routes approvals at the right moments in your processes
  • Drives coordinated actions across workflows
  • Helps deliver the right experiences to customers

Operational Support

  • Acts as an enterprise teammate for customer experience and marketing professionals
  • Supports campaign launches and real-time customer signal responses
  • Keeps teams organized and efficient

CX Enterprise Coworker leverages AI to enhance collaboration and streamline decision-making across your customer experience operations, making it easier for teams to execute coordinated strategies at scale.
