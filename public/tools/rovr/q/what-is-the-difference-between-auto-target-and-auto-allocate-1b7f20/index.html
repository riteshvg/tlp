What is the difference between Auto Target and Auto Allocate?

Auto-Target vs Auto-Allocate in Adobe Target

Both are automated traffic allocation features, but they serve different purposes and use different optimization approaches.

Auto-Allocate

Purpose

Identifies a winner among two or more experiences and automatically reallocates more traffic to the best-performing experience while the test continues.

How It Works

  • Continuously monitors performance of all experiences
  • Automatically increases traffic to higher-performing experiences
  • Decreases traffic to lower-performing experiences
  • Maintains some traffic to all experiences for ongoing learning
  • Focuses on conversion optimization based on your chosen metric

Best For

  • A/B testing scenarios where you want to find and promote the winning experience
  • Situations where you want faster results than manual A/B testing
  • Cases where you're willing to stop testing lower performers early
  • Reducing lost conversions by shifting traffic to winners in real-time

Key Benefit

Delivers results faster and generates higher revenue than manual A/B testing by serving the winning experience more often while the activity is still running.

Auto-Target

Purpose

Uses advanced machine learning to personalize content for each individual visitor by selecting from multiple high-performing experiences based on their unique profile and behavior.

How It Works

  • Builds predictive models using machine learning algorithms
  • Analyzes individual customer profiles and past visitor behaviors
  • Serves the most tailored experience to each specific visitor
  • Delivers personalized experiences rather than a single "winner"
  • Requires multiple experiences (typically 2-6) to personalize from

Best For

  • Personalization scenarios where different experiences work better for different audiences
  • Advanced optimization beyond simple winner selection
  • Situations where you want to serve the right experience to the right person
  • Maximizing conversions through individual-level customization

Key Benefit

Drives higher conversions by personalizing experiences to match each visitor's individual characteristics, not just serving one winning experience to everyone.

Side-by-Side Comparison

| Factor | Auto-Allocate | Auto-Target |

|--------|---------------|------------|

| Primary Goal | Find & promote a winner | Personalize for each visitor |

| Traffic Allocation | Shifts to top performer | Personalized per visitor |

| Machine Learning | Statistical analysis | Advanced predictive ML |

| Output | One winning experience | Multiple personalized experiences |

| Best Use Case | A/B testing with faster results | Individual-level personalization |

| Experiences Needed | 2+ | 2-6 |

| License Required | Target Standard or Premium | Target Premium only |

| A4T Support | Yes | Yes |

Real-World Example

Auto-Allocate: Testing 3 email subject lines. The system identifies Subject Line B has the highest click-through rate and gradually sends 70% of traffic to it, while still testing the others.

Auto-Target: Testing 3 different homepage layouts. The system learns that Layout A works best for mobile users, Layout B for desktop, and Layout C for returning customers—and serves each visitor the experience predicted to convert them best.

Choosing Between Them

  • Use Auto-Allocate if your primary goal is to identify and promote a single winner quickly
  • Use Auto-Target if your goal is to maximize conversions through personalization to individual visitors

Both are available with Analytics for Target (A4T) reporting integration.
