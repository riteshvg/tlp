What permissions or roles are required to use Sandbox Tooling for moving packages?

Permissions & Roles Required for Sandbox Tooling

Based on the available documentation context, here is what is confirmed regarding access requirements for Sandbox Tooling:

General Access Requirements

  • You must have access to Sandbox Tooling in your organization with the correct permissions enabled.
  • Only Product Administrators or System Administrators can assign sandboxes to roles.
  • Sandboxes are made available to users through roles assigned in the Permissions product.

Key Terminology to Understand

| Action | Who Can Do It | Where |

|---|---|---|

| Use a sandbox (switch to it) | Any user with sandbox access | Sandbox switcher in the UI |

| Assign sandboxes to roles | Product/System Admins only | Permissions product |

| Create/Reset/Delete sandboxes | Admins only | Adobe Experience Platform |

What the Documentation Confirms

  • Ensure you have access to Sandbox Tooling in your organization with the correct permissions before creating or importing packages.
  • Sandboxes must be assigned through roles in the Permissions product before users can access them.

⚠️ Limitation of This Answer

The retrieved documentation does not explicitly list the specific permission names (e.g., "Manage Sandboxes," "Export Sandbox," etc.) required for Sandbox Tooling operations. For the exact granular permissions needed (such as `Sandbox Administration` or `Package Management`), I would recommend:

1. Navigating to Admin Console → Adobe Experience Platform → Permissions

2. Reviewing the Sandboxes permission category for your role

3. Checking with your System Administrator to confirm the correct role has sandbox tooling permissions enabled

General Best Practice for Admins

When setting up roles for Sandbox Tooling access, ensure the role includes:

  • Access to the relevant sandboxes (both source and target)
  • Appropriate Data Collection permissions if packages include Tags properties
  • Permissions to view, create, and manage packages within the Sandboxes section of AEP
