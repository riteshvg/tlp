What is Adobe Analytics?

Adobe Analytics is a powerful web analytics solution that enables organizations to gather data and gain actionable insights from any digital customer interaction. It's designed to help businesses measure, analyze, and optimize their digital marketing activities.

Key Capabilities

Real-Time Data & Advanced Analytics

  • Access to real-time data about customer behavior across websites and apps
  • In-depth analysis capabilities and versatile reporting
  • Predictive intelligence to forecast future trends

Complex Analysis Tools

  • Ad hoc analysis in Analysis Workspace for flexible exploration of your data
  • Flow analysis to visualize customer journeys
  • Advanced segmentation to focus on specific subsets of your data
  • Anomaly detection using machine learning
  • Attribution modeling to understand how different touchpoints impact conversions

Marketing & Attribution Insights

  • Understand where customers interact with your brand
  • Identify which channels customers prefer
  • Determine which experiences resonate with your audience
  • See how different interactions throughout the customer journey affect conversion using machine learning and advanced statistical models

Audience Segmentation

  • Build, manage, share, and apply powerful audience segments across your reports
  • Integrate segments with Adobe Target, Adobe Journey Optimizer, and other Adobe products
  • Create codified audience insights that your organization can leverage

Who Uses It

While anyone in an organization can use Adobe Analytics to gain insights about customer behavior, it's designed with data analysts in mind. However, it serves multiple roles including administrators, analysts, end users, and developers.

Adobe Analytics is the foundation for building better customer experiences through data-driven decision making and improved ROI on your digital transformation initiatives.
