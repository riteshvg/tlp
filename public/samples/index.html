Welcome message

Thank you for visiting the Playground. This section demonstrates my learning of creating different pages using plain JavaScript methods. Access the pages from below or from the header menu. All the experimental pages are designed from the lessons by Chris Ferdinandi on theleanwebclub.com

As a developer/implementation consultant, I come across various use cases where I need a ready-to-use platform for testing different approaches. Think of it as a practice ground that athletes use for sharpening their skills. This is the reason behind ‘Playground’. A well designed area for me to test out different implementation requirements or keep practicing new skills. The page designs will differ with each scenarios/topics. And that is intentional to keep the muse happy.
